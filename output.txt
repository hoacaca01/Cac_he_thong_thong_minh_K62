From the west to the east the topography of quang binh is narrow and steep. 85 percent of the natural area is hilly. The whole area is divided into basic ecological zones high mountains hills and midlands plains coastal sandy areas. 

The topography is mainly narrow and steep, inclined from west to east, hills and mountains occupy 85% of the province's area and is strongly dissected. Almost the entire western region of the province is mountainous with a height of 1000-1500 m of which the highest is the phi co phi peak at 2017 m followed by low hills distributed in an upside-down bowl pattern. Near the coast is a small and narrow plain. Lastly the coastal sands are crescent-shaped or fan-shaped.
